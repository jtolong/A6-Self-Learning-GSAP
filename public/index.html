Why GSAP

GSAP gives precise control of timing, easing, and sequencing. It is designed for performance and pairs well with scroll based storytelling.

  • Timelines to chain animations
  • ScrollTrigger to sync motion with scroll
  • Robust easing and staggering

Pinned Visual

As you scroll, the image stays fixed while these paragraphs move. This helps focus on one visual while the narrative unfolds.

You can present steps, comparisons, or layered explanations while the media remains in view.

Pinning is handled by ScrollTrigger with a single config.
